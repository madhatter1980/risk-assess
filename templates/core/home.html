TIMEOUT for SAFETY

  • Stop and Think Through the Task.
  • Inspect Your Work Area and Equipment.
  • Identify Potential Hazards.
  • Implement Hazard Controls.
  • Do the Task Safely.

Benefits of Timeouts

Taking a moment to pause can save lives and prevent risks in the workplace.

Improved Awareness

Timeouts provide an opportunity to identify potential hazards that may otherwise go unnoticed, ensuring everyone is prepared and alert for the task ahead.

Better Decision Making

Pausing to assess the situation allows individuals to evaluate risks, consider safer options, and make informed decisions that prioritize personal safety over shortcuts.

Reduced Accidents

Proactive safety measures help minimise workplace accidents, creating a safer environment for everyone and reducing costly interruptions.
